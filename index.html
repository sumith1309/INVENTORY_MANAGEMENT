Key Features

Advanced AI capabilities for inventory optimization

ABC Classification

Automatically classify inventory items into A, B, and C categories based on annual consumption value. Top 70% → A, Next 20% → B, Remaining → C.

AI-Powered Prediction

Decision Tree classifier predicts ABC categories using Annual Usage, Unit Cost, Lead Time, and Past Demand with high accuracy.

Demand Forecasting

Linear Regression model predicts future demand from historical usage patterns, enabling proactive inventory management.

Safety Stock Calculation

Dynamic safety stock calculation using formula Z.σ√Lead Time where σ is 10% of predicted demand, ensuring optimal inventory levels.

Service Level Trade-offs

Interactive visualization showing the relationship between service levels, safety stock, and holding costs for informed decision-making.

Real-time Analytics

Dynamic charts and visualizations update in real-time as you modify input values, providing instant insights.

How It Works

Step-by-step process of inventory optimization

1

ABC Classification

Calculate annual value (usage × unit cost), sort items by value, and classify into A, B, or C categories based on cumulative percentage.

2

AI Classification

Train a Decision Tree classifier using item features (Annual Usage, Unit Cost, Lead Time, Past Demand) to predict ABC categories.

3

Demand Forecasting

Use Linear Regression to predict future demand from past demand patterns, enabling accurate inventory planning.

4

Safety Stock Calculation

Calculate optimal safety stock using the formula: Z.σ√Lead Time where σ = 10% of predicted demand and Z corresponds to service level.

5

Trade-off Analysis

Visualize service level trade-offs to balance customer satisfaction, inventory risk, and financial impact.
